Our activity

We Buy coffee from farmers who harvest their crops in stages. They use the method of collecting coffee beans and then drying them on the roofs of houses, after cleaning them of impurities and separating the husks from the coffee kernel, to prepare the wish coffee drink that Yemenis like to drink in the morning. The coffee beans are roasted by local laboratories, then packaged for sale, and finally, we produce and ship.

Why is our coffee your best choice?

Because it is extracted from the finest Yemeni coffee beans, the "homeland of coffee" on which the sun never sets.
Each coffee bean has a story that is specifically designed to produce you a cup of coffee with love, and because every coffee lover has a unique taste and different mood, our coffee beans have been carefully selected and customized to suit every taste.

The Doroob Almokha Coffee Extracted from the lands of these good areas: (Khawlan, Ibb al-Khadra, Haraz, Bani Fadl, Bani Matar, al-Hayma, Bani Hammad, and other Yemeni regions). This makes its taste rich, pleasant, and concentrated, and its distinctive and strong aroma can attract you from meters away.
